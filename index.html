ERIC RIEDEL

Student · Developer · Designer

Hi there, I'm Eric Riedel. I'm a senior Computer Science student at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor, Michigan. I enjoy designing and building things for the web.

I love talking about movies, sports, technology, robotics, comedy, puppies, the plausibility of conspiracy theories, and pretty much anything else you want to pick my brain about.

I love being creative and expressing that creativity through the things I make for the web,

I am very passionate about speedcubing and the community surrounding that hobby. Solving my Rubibk's cube or another puzzle helps me to clear my mind and relax after a long day.

I am a huge animal lover, and I spend time each week volunteering at local animal shelters, particularly the Midwest Rabbit Rescue in Plymouth, MI, which fosters local abandoned rabbits.
